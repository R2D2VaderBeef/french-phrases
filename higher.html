About

This page was created to help people revise for the Higher French Directed Writing exam.

I selected some phrases from the Directed Writing booklet, just based on which ones I liked.
Because of this, you should not rely on the site as a complete revision tool.
Also, the topics are taken directly from the booklet, so some phrases aren't in the topic you'd expect.
